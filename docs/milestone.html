Result

Our result is a movable character that is controlled with the typical movement keys WASD. W and S control forward and backward movement, while A and D are for left and right.

The player can choose to shoot a first portal in the scene with either the left or right mouse button.

Shooting both portals will result in the ability to view the opposite portal's perspective through either portal.
